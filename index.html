POMERIAN

Pomeranians are small, intelligent dogs with fluffy double coats and lively personalities. They're confident and curious but require regular exercise and grooming. With a lifespan of 12-16 years, they're popular pets known for their alertness and charming appearance, making them great companions for attentive owners and families.

GERMAN SHEPERD

Big bags for big fans. The German Shepherd is a versatile and intelligent breed, known for its loyalty and protective nature. These medium to large-sized dogs have a distinctive appearance, excel in various roles like police and service work, and require regular exercise. They have a lifespan of 9-13 years and are popular worldwide for their versatility and loyalty.

HUSKY

Siberian Huskies are medium-sized dogs known for their striking appearance, including striking blue eyes and a thick double coat. They are social, energetic, and independent, requiring ample exercise and mental stimulation. Originating from Siberia, they have a lifespan of 12-15 years and are popular pets, but they need committed owners to meet their active needs.
